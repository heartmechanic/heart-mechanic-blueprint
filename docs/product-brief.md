# Product Brief

## Name

The Heart Mechanic Blueprint

## MVP 1 Promise

A progressive, course-paced experience for building the first visible architecture of a user's Kingdom.

## Core Experience

Users move through Weeks 1-3 only:

- Week 1: build the Kingdom population with the Census
- Week 2: add the Foundation Layer for Kingdom members
- Week 3: place members into the first Kingdom structure

Weeks 4-6 are locked.

## Week 3 Structure

- Inner Counsel
- Knights
- Nobles
- Courtiers
- Villagers

## Design Direction

Premium, calm, minimal and emotionally intelligent. More luxury wellbeing product than spreadsheet. Avoid mystical, childish or overly colorful design language.

Relationships appear as spheres. The Kingdom Map is the destination view users build toward progressively.
